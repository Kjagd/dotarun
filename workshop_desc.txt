[h1] If you liked Mario Kart / Crash Team Racing / Lego Racers you'll love this! [/h1]

Get to the finish line faster than your opponents by using all the dirty tricks you can come up with.

Use your trusted force staff and the additional items and abilities you collect by finding the marked squares, but be careful of the traps!

Each item and spell can only be used once, so use it right!

The map is FFA and the first to score 30 points wins. You get 10 points for being 1st, 7 for being 2nd, 5 for being 3rd, 4 for being 4th, 3 for being 5th, 2 for being 6th and anyone else gets 1 point if they finish.
