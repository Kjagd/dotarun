Lobby password: dotarun

If you liked Mario Kart / Crash Team Racing / Lego Racers you'll love this!

Get to the finish line faster than your opponents by using all the dirty tricks you can come up with using your trusted
force staff and the addional items and abilities you collect by finding the marked squares.

But be careful of the traps...

The mod is open sourced here: https://github.com/Kjagd/dotarun
